About the shelter

Kamloops OOTC Shelter Society provides shelter and support with an Indigenous lens during the coldest and hottest months of the year. 47% of Kamloops citizens experiencing homelessness identify as Indigenous. OOTC focuses on offering Indigenous food security, cultural connection, and access to Elders from various bands.

Service area: Serving Kamloops, BC, and surrounding areas.

Programs & support

Emergency Shelter

Overnight shelter during extreme cold and heat months, with Indigenous cultural support.

Food Security

Access to Indigenous foods and meals that respect cultural traditions.

Elder & Cultural Access

Connection to Elders from various bands for guidance and cultural activities.
